Shahbaz Ahmed IPL Profile

Shahbaz Ahamad is an Indian cricketer who plays for the Bangalore franchise in the Indian Premier League. Shahbaz Ahamad was born on Dec 12, 1994; and as of 2023, he is 29 years old. Shahbaz Ahamad is an all-rounder from Mewat, Haryana and bats left handed and bowls orthodox. He made his IPL debut in 2020.

Shahbaz Ahamad has played 39 matches to date in his IPL career and has scored 321 runs, with an average of 16.89. With his highest IPL score being 45 runs. Shahbaz Ahamad has hit 21 fours and 12 sixes in his IPL career.

In the IPL Auction 2023, the Bangalore franchise acquired the services of Shahbaz Ahamad for Rs 2.40 Cr.

On May 2023, he played his last IPL match against Hyderabad at the Rajiv Gandhi International Stadium, Hyderabad, India, scoring 0 runs off 0 balls.
